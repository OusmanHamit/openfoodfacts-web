Prispevajte k Open Food Facts

Vse, kar potrebujete, je skeniranje! :-)

Ali lahko živilsko industrijo naredimo bolj odprto in pregledno? Seveda, (skeniramo!) 25.000 Open Food Facts contributors and growing - Since 2012

Vsakdo lahko prispeva

Bodite del naše skupne, brezplačne in odprto zbirke podatkov o prehrambenih izdelkih iz celega sveta!

Open Food Facts je neprofitni projekt, ki so ga v celoti izdelali prostovoljci, potrebujemo vas.

Dodajte izdelke

Use our Android, iPhone or Windows Phone app to easily scan the barcode of products from your home or local stores and upload pictures of their label.

Nimate pametnega telefona? Ni problema: s fotoaparatom lahko dodate tudi izdelke neposredno na spletno mesto.

Povej svetu

Potrebno je kategorizirati izdelke in izvleči sezname sestavin in prehranskih dejstev, da analiziramo njihovo prehransko kakovost, ugotovimo, ali so primerni za vegane in vegetarijance in še veliko več!

You can help us complete products by selecting and cropping photos and filling in information.

Povej svetu

Ali vam je všeč Open Food Facts? Povej drugim o tem!

You can present the project to your family and friends, show them how to install the app and contribute, write a blog post, and share Open Food Facts on social media.

Naj bo lokalno

You can help translate the site and mobile app in your language, as well as the taxonomies, and to translate presentations, announcements etc.

Start or join a local contributors community: add local products, recruit friends, present the project in local meetups and conferences etc.

Potrebna je strokovna pomoč

Razvijanje Open Food Facts zahteva tudi specializirano znanje in strokovno znanje na številnih različnih področjih:

  • Vodenje projektov

    We have tons of ideas and you probably have even more, but it is quite a challenge to prioritize them, to build a roadmap, and to manage projects when every participant is a volunteer with often limited time available.

  • Oblikovanje

    We need help to build a better user experience on the Open Food Facts web site and mobile app, to improve their design, to create impactful presentation materials etc.

  • Razvoj

    We have a lot of development work to do. On the Open Food Facts backend (Perl and MongoDB), API (JSON), web site (templatized HTML5, JS, Foundation), iOS and Android apps (a Java/Kotlin version on Android, and a Swift version on iOS, both needing volunteers), a Flutter app for iOS and Android, but also to build new cool reuses etc. We have projects in many programming languages to ensure anyone can reuse and contribute to Open Food Facts, in any language. We also have a growing Artificial Intelligence effort to simplify contribution work. Our code is on GitHub.

  • Gradnja skupnosti

    Zgraditi moramo lokalne skupnosti v vseh državah in jih hkrati združiti po vsem svetu.

    It is very difficult to bootstrap a local community in a country without living there, so your help to find the first very motivated participants is essential. Ste eden izmed njih?

  • Komunikacija

    Nismo prepričani, kaj bi napisali tukaj, bi lahko pomagali?

    More seriously, there are lot of cool things that users, contributors and reusers do with Open Food Facts, it would be great to get more people to know about it. In particular, we need help for public and media relations.

  • Posebni projekti

    There are lots of interesting and original applications of food open data that we could work on with government food agencies, food producers, researchers, universities, schools, NGOs etc. Mogoče že imate ideje? Pomagajte nam, da jih spodbudimo naprej.

Pogovorimo se!

V naši skupnosti sodelavcev je veliko idej, energije in navdušenja, pridružite se nam!

Tu je nekaj mest, kjer lahko srečate druge sodelavce, se z njimi pogovarjate in sodelujete:

  • Slack

    Slack je najboljši način za druženje in sodelovanje z drugimi sodelavci, razvijalci in ponovnimi uporabniki. It's a discussion forum you can access from your browser or your phone. Popolnoma se je spremenilo, kako sodelujemo. Please try it, click on the button below to get invited to our Slack.

  • Skupine Facebook

    We have Facebook groups for Open Food Facts contributors in many languages. Joining them is a good way to get news about the project and to share announcements to a wider audience.

  • Wiki

    We also have a wiki that we use to collaboratively document Open Food Facts and its sub-projects. From the wiki, you can learn how to help translate the multilingual information (categories, labels etc.) a food product might have (using what we call taxonomies). You can even help add a food category or a label that is not yet supported by Open Food Facts.

Obstaja veliko več načinov, kako prispevati k Open Food Facts. Pridružite se nam na strani Slack in začnimo razpravo!
