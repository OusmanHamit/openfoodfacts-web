Open Products Facts

Open Products Facts està en construcció.

It is based on Open Food Facts and made by the same team. Estem en procés de fer canvis per tenir en compte els aspectes específics dels aliments per a mascotes. Uniu-vos al nostre Slack al canal #openproductsfacts per debatre i desenvolupar més Open Products Facts!

Descobrir

Open Products Facts is a general products (food, cosmetics and pet food exempted) database made by everyone, for everyone.

You can use it to make better choices about products, and as it is open data, anyone can re-use it for any purpose.

Contribuir

Open Products Facts is a non-profit project developed by thousands of volunteers from around the world. Podeu començar a col·laborar afegint un producte que no sigui alimentari, ni per a mascotes i que no sigui cosmètic; tenim molts projectes interessants als quals podeu contribuir de moltes maneres diferents.
